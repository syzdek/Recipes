Seasoned Pumpkin Seeds

Ingredients:
   1-2 Pumpkin
   2 tbls. butter
   2 tsp.  salt (divided)
   1 tsp.  garlic powder
   1 tsp.  onion powder
   1/2 tsp. cayenne powder

Directions:
   1) Boil seeds in salted water for 10 minutes.
   2) While seeds boil, melt butter and mix in salt, garlic powder, onion
      powder, and cayenne powder.
   3) Drain seeds and coat in seasoned butter.
   4) Place seeds in a single layer on a baking sheet.
   5) Bake at 325 degrees for 20-30 minutes or until golden. Stir occasionally.
   6) Let cool paper towel covered plate.
