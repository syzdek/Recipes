Whipped Cream

Ingredients:
   1 C        heavy whipping cream
   1/4 C      confectioner's sugar

   One of the following spices:
   2 tbl      maple syrup
   1 tsp      cinnamon, ground
   1 tsp      vanilla
   

Directions:

   Combine heavy whipping cream, confectioner's sugar, and preferred spice.
   Beat until peaks form.
