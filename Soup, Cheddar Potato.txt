Cheddar Potato Soup
-------------------

Ingredients:
       1 gal  milk, whole
       5 lb   russet potatoes, cubed to 1/2"
       3 lb   cheddar cheese, grated
       4 tbl  butter
       3 ---  onions, yellow chopped
       2 tbl  garlic, minced
       1 cup  Heavy Cream
       2 cup  Sourc Cream
       2 tsp  salt
       1 tsp  black pepper
     1/2 tsp  cayenne pepper, ground

Directions:
   1. Using a large stock pot, Sauté onions in butter until carmelized.
   2. Add garlic and continue sautéing until fragrant.
   3. Add milk, heavy cream, sour cream, salt, pepper, and cayenne.
   4. Whisk until combined.
   5. Reduce heat to medium and add cubed potatoes.
   6. Bring to a simmer and reduce to medium low to low.
   7. Cook until potatoes are tender (about 30 minutes).
   8. Turn off heat.  While stirring, sprinkle cheese in a little a time. Adding
      more cheese once the existing cheese is melted into the soup.
   9. Serve hot.
