Pancakes

Ingredients:
   1 1/2 cup  milk
   1/4 cup    white vinegar
   2 tsp      vanilla extract
   4 tbl      butter, melted
   2 large    eggs
   2 cup      flour
   1/2 cup    sugar
   2 tsp      baking powder
   1 tsp      baking soda
   3/4 tsp    salt

Directions:
   1) Combine the milk and vinegar in a small bowl for 10-12 minutes and set
      it aside.
   2) Melt butter on stove top.
   3) In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and
      salt.
   4) In small bowl, whisk together egg, milk, melted butter, and vanilla
      extract.
   5) Combine wet and dry ingredients, but do not over mix. Let sit for 2-3
      minutes.
   6) Spoon about 1/4 cup of batter onto skillet and flip once lightly brown.
