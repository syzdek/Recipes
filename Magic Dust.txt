Magic Dust

Ingredients:
   1/2 C  paprika
   1/4 C  kosher salt, finely ground
   1/4 C  sugar
   1/4 C  chili powder
   1/4 C  ground cumin
   1/4 C  granulated garlic
   2 tbl  mustard powder
   2 tbl  ground black pepper
   2 tbl  cayenne

Directions:
   1) Mix all ingredients.
   2) Store in air tight jar or shaker.

Notes:
   1) Keep on table to spice up dishes.
