Biscuits & Gravy

Ingredients:
   Biscuits:
   about 3/4 cups milk
   1 tbl. white vinegar
   2 cups sifted flour
   2 tsp. baking powder
   1 tsp. baking soda
   1/2 tsp. salt
   2 tbl. sugar
   4 tsp. butter

   Gravy:
   1 lb Sausage
   2 oz Whiskey
   Onion Powder
   Flour
   Milk
   Salt & Pepper

Directions:
   1) Add vinegar to milk and let sit for 10 minutes.
   2) Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and sugar.
   3) Cut in butter.
   4) Add soured milk gradually, stirring until soft dough is formed.
   5) Turn out on slightly floured board and lightly knead dough.
   6) Roll 1/2 inch thick and cut with 2 inch floured biscuit cutter.
   7) Bake on ungreased sheet in a 400 degree oven for 12-15 minutes.
