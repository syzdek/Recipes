Parmesan Spinach Cream Sauce

Ingredients:
     1-2 tbl  cooking oil
       2 ---  onions, coarsely chopped
       1 lb   sausage, thinly sliced
       2 cup  mushrooms, sliced (optional)
       6 tbl  garlic, minced
       3 cup  spinach, chopped
       3 cup  cream, heavy
      16 tbl  salted butter
       1 cup  parmesan cheese, grated
         ---  noodles (i.e. angle hair or fettuccine
         ---  salt, pepper, and seasonings to taste

Directions:
   1. Heat oil in deep sided skillet over medium to medium high heat
   2. Add chopped onions and sliced sausage. Cook until onions are caramelized
      and edges of sausage are browned.
   3. Add sliced mushrooms and cook until mushrooms are tender and any moisture
      has evaporated.
   4. Reduce heat to medium to medium low, add minced garlic, and cooking until
      fragrant (about a minute).
   5. Add chopped spinach and cook until wilted.
   6. Reduce heat to medium low and add heavy cream and bring to a simmer while
      stirring.
   7. Add butter to skillet and return to a simmer while continuing to stir. 
   8. Reduce heat to low and sprinkle parmesan cheese over sauce and then
      stir suace until cheese is incorporated and sauce is smooth.
   9. Stir in salt, pepper, and any additional seasonings.
  10. Remove from heat and mix into noodles.

Notes:

   * I use a cast iron skillet.
   * I start boiling the water when I start heating the skillet.  The sauce
     is done around the same time as the noodles.
