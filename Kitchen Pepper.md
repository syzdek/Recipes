Kitchen Pepper
==============

Ingredients
-----------
        1 oz   ginger, ground
      1/2 oz   black pepper
      1/2 oz   cinnamon, ground
      1/2 oz   cloves, ground
      1/2 oz   nutmeg, ground
        6 oz   salt

Directions
----------
   1) Mix well.
   2) Keep dry.
