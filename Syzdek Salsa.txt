Syzdek Salsa

Ingredients:
   1 Medium sized sweet onion
   1 lime
   3-4 roma tomatoes
   14 oz jar of mild peppadews
   2-3 cups of large fresh green olives

Directions:
   1) Dice peppadews, tomatoes, onion, and olives.
   2) Cut limes into slices and sqeeze juice into mixture.
   3) Stir ingredients.
   4) Best if let set over night in refrigerator before serving.

Source: David M. Syzdek
