Cole Slaw

Ingredients:
       1 cup  mayonnaise
       4 tsp  vinegar, white
     1/4 cup  sugar, granulated
     1/4 tsp  dry mustard
     1/4 tsp  salt
       1 tsp  black pepper, ground
       1 tsp  dill, dried
       1 ---  head of cabbage, shredded
     2-3 ---  whole carrots, shredded

Directions:
   1) Whisk mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, mustard, salt, pepper, and dill.
   2) Mix dressing, shredded cabbage, and shredded carrots.
   3) Cover and refrigerate for 2-24 hors.
