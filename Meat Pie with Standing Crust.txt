Meat Pie with Standing Crust
----------------------------

Crust Ingredients:
   2 1/2 cup  flour
     1/2 cup  water
       3 tbl  butter
       3 tbl  lard
     1/2 tsp  salt
         ---  egg wash

Filling Ingredients:

   Cheshire Port Pie
       1 lb   pork loin, cubed
       1 ---  apple, chopped
       2 tbl  sugar
       1 tsp  salt
     1/2 tsp  pepper
     1/8 tsp  nutmeg
       
   Beef and Onion Pie
     1/2 lb   beef, cubed
       1 ---  onion, chopped and sauted
       2 tbl  garlic, minced
       2 tsp  flour
       1 tsp  salt
     1/2 tsp  pepper


Directions:
   1. Bring water to a simmer in a sauce pan.
   2. Melt the remaining crust ingredients into the water and  remove from heat.
   3. In a mixing bowl, combine flour and water mixture.
   4. Knead for 5-10 minutes.
   5. Roll dough into a log.
   6. Divide into two parts, remove a walnut size of dough from each part.
   7. Flatten the two larger parts into thick disks and roll the two smaller
      parts into thinner disks about an inch bigger in diameter than the pie..
   8. Cover and let cool for 4 hours.
   9. Flour the bottom of a jar or bottle and form the thick disks of dough
      into bowls or coffins around the base of the jar or bottle.
  10. Carefully remove the jar or bottle from the dough coffin.
  11. In a bowl, mix the filling ingredients and then tightly pack each coffin
      of dough with the filling leaving 1/2" - 3/4" at the top.
  12. Slightly warm up the thin disks of dough until pliable and brush egg wash
      around the edges of the disk.
  13. Cover the pie with the thin disks with edges with the egg wash curling up
      and facing the dough coffin, then squeeze the lip of the lid and the
      coffin together to seal.
  14. Brush egg wash around the outside of the pie and cut two small slits into
      the lids of the pie.
  15. Bake for 60 - 90 minutes at 375 F.
