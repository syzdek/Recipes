Pizza Sauce

Ingredients:
   6     oz  tomato paste
   12    oz  tomato sauce
   4     tbl parmesan cheese
   1     tsp onion powder
   1     tsp dried oregano
   1     tsp dried basil
   1/2   tsp black pepper
   2     tsp minced garlic
   2     tbl honey

Directions:
   1) Combine all ingredients in small bowl.
   2) Let sit at least 2 hours to allow flavors to blend
