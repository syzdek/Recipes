Stuffing

Ingredients:
       1 ---  loaf of Challah
       1 cup  celery, chopped
       1 cup  sweet onions, chopped (about 2 onions)
       3      sticks of butter (24 tbl)
    4-6       roasted garlic cloves
   2 1/4 tsp  salt
   1 1/2 tsp  black pepper
       2 tsp  sage, ground (7-8 leafs of fresh sage, minced)
       2 tsp  dried rosemary (4 tsp fresh roasmary, chopped)
   1 1/2 tsp  dried thyme (3 tsp fresh thyme, chopped)
       1 tsp  marjoram, ground
     1/2 tsp  nutmeg, ground
   1 1/2 cup  chicken stock
       2 --   large eggs, beaten


Directions:
   1) Cube bread (should make 13 or so cups) and let dry for 24-48 hours.
   2) Saute onion and celery in 2-3 tbl of butter for 10-12 minutes until
      onion and celery are tender.
   3) Add remaining butter and melt.
   4) Mash roasted garlic into a paste.
   5) Mix garlic and seasonings into onions and celery.
   6) Combine eggs, chicken stock, celery, and onions.
   7) Mix in bread cubes.
   8) Spread in lightly greased casserole dish (9x13).
   9) Bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes.
