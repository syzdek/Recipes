Thyme and Cheese Biscuits

Ingredients:
   2 cups flour
   3 tsp. baking powder
   1 tsp. fresh minced thyme
   1/2 tsp. minced fresh parsley
   1/2 tsp. minced fresh rosemary
   1/2 cup cheddar cheese
   5 tablespoons butter
   1/2 cup milk

Directions:
   1) Preheat over to 400 degrees.
   2) Grease a cookie sheet and set aside.
   3) Mix flour, baking powder, herbs and cheese in a large mixing bowl,
      using a fork.
   4) Cut in the butter. Mixture will be crumbly.
   5) Add the milk and stir until dough holds together, you may add more
      milk if necessary.
   6) Drop by large spoonfuls on the cookie sheet an inch apart.
   7) Bake 10-12 minutes.
