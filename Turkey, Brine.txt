Turkey Brine
============

Ingredients
-----------
       3 gal  cold water
       3 cup  apple juice or apple cider
       2 cup  brown sugar
   1 1/2 cup  kosher salt
       7 tbl  minced garlic
       2 tbl  black pepper, coarse ground
       2 tbl  rosemary leaves, dried
       5 ---  whole bay leaves
       1 ---  lemon
       1 ---  onion

Directions:
   1. Bring water to boil.
   2. Add  apple juice, brown sugar, salt, garlic, black pepper, rosemary, and
      bay leaves.
   3. Using potato peeler, peel lemon. Thinly slice lemon and remove seeds. Add
      lemon peel and slices to brine.
   4. Cut onion into 8 chunks and add to brine (skin and all).
   5. Return to boil, stirring to dissole salt and sugar.
   6. Allow brine to cool to room temperature.
   7. Place uncooked turkey in large brining bag or pot. pour in the brine
      solutiion to cover the turkey.
   8. Refrigerate for 16 - 24 hours.
   9. Remove turkey from brine and submerge in cool fresh water for 15 minutes
      to remove excess salt.
  10. Remove turkey from cold water, pat dry, and cook as normal.
