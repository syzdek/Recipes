Blue Cheese Dressing

Ingredients:
     3/4 cup  mayonnaise
     1/2 cup  sour cream
     1/2 cup  buttermilk
   1 1/2 tsp  garlic powder
       1 tsp  sugar
       1 tsp  salt
       2 tsp  vinegar
       1 tps  Worcestershire sauce
     1/4 tsp  black pepper, cracked
     1/2 cup  blue cheese, crumbled

Directions:
   1. Add all ingredients except for blue cheese to a bowl
   2. Whisk until ingredients are combined
   3. Fold in blue cheese
