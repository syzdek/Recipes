US Units (Volume)
=================

      From        |       To              
----------------- | --------------  
   3 teaspoons    |  1 tablespoon   
   2 tablespoons  |  1 fluid ounce  
   4 tablespoons  |  1/4 cup        
   1 shot         |  1 fluid ounce  
   8 fluid ounces |  1 cup          
   2 cups         |  1 pint         
   2 pints        |  1 quart        
   4 quarts       |  1 gallon       
   4 gallons      |  1 bucket       
   2 buckets      |  1 bushel       
15.5 gallons      |  1 keg
