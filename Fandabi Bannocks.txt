Fandabi Bannocks
----------------

Base Ingredients:
       1 cup  oats (plus extra)
       2 cup  flour (plus extra)
       1 cup  fat (suet, lard, coconut oil, etc)
     2-6 tbl  hot water

Flavoring Additives:

   Cinnamon Spice:
        1/4 cup  raisens
          1 tbl  brown sugar
          1 tsp  cinnamon
        1/4 tsp  nutmeg
        1/4 tsp  all spice
        1/4 tsp  cloves

   Peanut Butter:
        1/4 cup  peanut butter
        1/8 cup  chopped peanuts
          2 tbl  sugar
                 * reduce fat to 3/4 cup

   Garlic Herb:
          1 tbl  sugar
          1 tsp  basil, dried
          1 tsp  parsley, dried
          1 tsp  garlic powder
          1 tsp  salt
        1/2 tsp  black pepper

Directions:
   1. Pre-heat oven to 400 F.
   2. Mix oats, flour, and flavoring additives. 
   3. Melt fat in sauce pan.
   4. Quickly mix fat into oat and flour mixuture until it resembles a crumble.
   5. Add small amounts of hot water while stirring until dough binds together.
   6. Knead until it doesn't stick to hands and can be formed into a bowl.
   7. Mix a little flour and oats in a separate bowl.
   8. Break off dough balls about the size of your fist and pat them into
      biscuits about as thick as your fingers and as wide as your hand.
   9. Pat extra flour/oats on each side of the biscuits.
  10. Bake for 10 minutes at 400 F.
  11. Reduce heat to 125 F and continue baking for 60 minutes.
  12. Remove from oven and transfer biscuits to cooling rack.
  13. Store in cool, dry, dark location.
